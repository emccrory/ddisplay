<b> In memoriam: Euhel Campbell</b>:  Former Fermilab employee Euhel J. Campbell, age 82, of Sweetwater, Tennessee, passed away on Tuesday, March 15, at his home. He was a technician in the Technical Division working on conventional magnet production. He worked at the laboratory from 1976 to 1998. Read Campbell&#8217;s obituary.
