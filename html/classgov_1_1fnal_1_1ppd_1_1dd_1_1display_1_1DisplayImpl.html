Detailed Description

The implementation of a Display. This is made concrete on the controller side through DisplayFacade, and on the display side through DisplayControllerMessagingAbstract (which is today (3/2017) made concrete as DisplayAsConnectionToFireFox)

Author
Elliott McCrory, Fermilab AD/Instrumentation
