Given a number, retrieve the Channel that corresponds to this from the database.

This assumes:

  • Negative numbers are lists of channels. (-channelNumber) is the index of the list from the database

Number bigger than a billion are images. (channeNumber - 1E9) is the index of the image from the database All other numbers (that is, between 0 and 1E9, exclusive) are assumed to be an index for the channel from the database
